**Tag** — tiny uppercase pill for topics / modalities. The grounding olive accent of the palette.

```jsx
<div style={{ display: 'flex', flexWrap: 'wrap', gap: 8 }}>
  <Tag>TCC</Tag><Tag>Mindfulness</Tag><Tag>Regulação Emocional</Tag>
</div>
```

- **tone**: `olive` (default), `terra`, `sand`. Always lay tags out in a `flex` row with `gap`.
