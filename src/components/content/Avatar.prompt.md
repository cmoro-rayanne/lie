**Avatar** — terracotta gradient circle with initials, or a cover-cropped photo.

```jsx
<Avatar initials="MS" />
<Avatar src="/photo.jpg" alt="Mariana S." size={56} />
```

- `size` sets the diameter (default 48px); initials scale with it.
- Pair with a name (sans 500) + role (sans 300 muted) to the right.
