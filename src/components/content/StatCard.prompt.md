**StatCard** — a quiet proof point (years of experience, clients seen, satisfaction). Stack a few in a credibility block.

```jsx
<StatCard value="500+" label="Clientes atendidos" sub="ao longo da carreira" />
<StatCard value="98%" label="Taxa de satisfação" sub="baseado em avaliações" />
```

- The metric renders in serif terracotta; the label is bold sans; `sub` is optional muted sans.
- Vertical terracotta→olive accent bar on the left; border warms on hover.
